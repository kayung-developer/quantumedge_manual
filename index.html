Welcome to QuantumEdge Terminal

This comprehensive user manual will guide you through every feature of the QuantumEdge Terminal, from initial setup to deploying advanced, AI-powered trading strategies. Our goal is to provide you with a powerful, intuitive, and robust tool for algorithmic trading.

Getting Started

1. First Launch & Activation

Upon launching the application, you will be prompted to activate your license using a Serial Key. This key connects you to the Marketplace server, allowing you to download strategies and verify your admin status.

2. The .env File (For AI Strategy)

To use the "AI Signal Confirmation" strategy, ensure your Python environment has tensorflow installed. The application will automatically detect and load the LSTM models from the models/ directory.

Risk & Money Management Math

This is the most critical section for long-term profitability. QuantumEdge uses Dynamic Position Sizing based on volatility (ATR).

1. Calculating the Stop Loss (SL)

The bot does not use fixed pips. It uses the Average True Range (ATR) to adapt to market speed.

Formula: SL_Distance = ATR * atr_sl_multiplier

Example: If ATR is 20 pips and multiplier is 2.0, SL is 40 pips. If the market slows down and ATR is 10 pips, SL tightens to 20 pips.

2. Calculating Take Profit (TP)

The Take Profit is derived from the Stop Loss using your Risk-Reward Ratio.

Formula: TP_Distance = SL_Distance * rr_ratio

Example: If SL is 40 pips and R:R is 1.5, TP is 60 pips.

3. Calculating Lot Size (Volume)

The bot calculates how many lots to buy so that if the SL is hit, you only lose exactly your defined Risk Percentage.

Formula: LotSize = (Equity * Risk%) / (SL_Points * TickValue)

4. The $100 Account Rule (Margin Auto-Scaler)

If your account is small (e.g., $100), the calculated Lot Size might be too small (0.001) or too large for your margin. The bot includes a Safety Shrinker:

  • If calculated risk volume is < 0.01 (Broker Min), it forces 0.01 Lots.
  • If volume requires more margin than you have, it shrinks the volume step-by-step until it fits your wallet.

Strategies: Forex & Crypto (Swing)

These strategies are designed for MetaTrader 5 and Binance. They operate on candle closes (e.g., H1, M15).

1. SuperTrend & ADX (Trend Filtered)

Best For: Trending Markets (Gold, Crypto, GBPJPY).
Logic: Enters when SuperTrend flips direction, BUT only if ADX > 25 (Strong Trend) and Price aligns with EMA 200.

ParameterDefaultDescription
st_period10SuperTrend Lookback Period
st_multiplier3.0SuperTrend Factor (Higher = Wider)
adx_threshold25Min ADX value to allow trades
trend_ema_period200Long-term trend filter

2. RSI & Bollinger (Regime Filtered)

Best For: Ranging Markets (EURGBP, AUDCAD).
Logic: Mean Reversion. Buys when price hits Lower Band + RSI Oversold.
Unique Feature: Uses an ADX Ceiling. It will NOT trade if ADX > 25 (Trend), protecting you from crashes.

ParameterDefaultDescription
bb_period20Bollinger Band Length
bb_std_dev2.0Standard Deviation (Width)
adx_ranging_threshold25Max ADX allowed (Must be calm)

3. Smart Money Concept (SMC)

Best For: Volatile Pairs (XAUUSD, BTCUSD).
Logic: Identifies "Order Blocks" and "Fair Value Gaps" (FVG) created by institutional moves. It places trades when price retraces into these zones, aligned with the EMA 200.

ParameterDefaultDescription
atr_multiplier2.5Used to define the width of the FVG zone

4. AI Signal Confirmation (LSTM)

Best For: All Pairs.
Logic: A Deep Learning Neural Network analyzes the last 60 candles (OHLCV). It outputs a probability (0-100%). We only trade if the AI Confidence > 70% AND the technical trend agrees.

ParameterDefaultDescription
confidence_threshold0.70Minimum AI confidence (0.0 to 1.0)

Strategies: Binary Options

These strategies are tuned for High Frequency (M1) trading and are ideal for generating signals for auto-clickers.

1. Binary: Bollinger Band Bounce

Logic: Reversal. Signals a PUT when price pierces the Upper Band, or CALL at Lower Band.
Price Action Filter: Rejects the trade if the candle body is massive (Momentum Blast), preventing losses during breakouts.

2. Binary: ADX Breakout

Logic: Trend Follow. Signals CALL when +DI crosses -DI, provided ADX > 30.
Confirmation: Requires a widening gap between DI lines to ensure buyer dominance.

3. Binary: RSI Extremes Reversal

Logic: Scalping. Signals reversal when RSI hits extreme levels (e.g., 90 or 10).
Pivot Check: Waits for the RSI to actually "hook" back (turn around) before signaling, to avoid catching a falling knife.

Visual Strategy Builder

Create your own strategies without coding. Drag, drop, and connect nodes.

  • Indicator Node: Source of data (RSI, Price, EMA).
  • Condition Node: Logic check (Greater Than, Crosses Over).
  • Action Node: The result (Buy/Sell).

Example: Connect RSI Node -> Condition (Below 30) -> Action (BUY).
